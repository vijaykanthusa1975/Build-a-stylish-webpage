Project Manager doesn't equal Scrum Master

Project Manager
Communicator, Process Knowledgeable, Detailed		 Scrum Master
Communicator, Servant Leader, Agile SME
  • Leads by timeline
  • Each Project has its own PM
  • Change Management Process
  • Technical knowledge not required
  • Assigns the work
  • Leads by removing barriers
  • Ongoing member of Scrum Team
  • Embrace and proactively manage change
  • Technical knowledge acquired as part of Scrum Team

  • Conduct PM Self Assessment
  • Verify Current or Aspirational Role in CE Portal is listed as Scrum Master
    • This will show the required training
    • Advise to take transformational first:

  • Get a mentor

Certifications

  • AT&T Certified Scrum Master
    • Internal to AT&T
    • 30 question test
    • 360 degree survey that maps to the skill set of a SM
  • PMI - Agile Certified Practitioner : Created for those who believe in and apply agile principles and practices on projects. It requires a combination of training, experience and an exam. It also bridges agile approaches such as SCRUM, XP, LEAN and Kanban.
  • Scrumalliance.org : Certified Scrum Master
